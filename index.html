The KID went to the grocery store. They were sent there by their parents to BUY healthy fruits and VEGETABLES. First they went to the produce section. They looked at RED apples, yellow bananas, and orange CARROTS, but then they had a better idea! They WALKED to the candy aisle and carefully studied all the sweet options. After much deliberation, they grabbed a BIG chocolate bar, colorful jelly beans, and long gummy worms.

After using MONEY from their parents to buy the candy, they walked to the bus stop. They unwrapped the chocolate bar while waiting for the bus. They opened the bag of jelly beans while riding the bus. They started EATING the gummy worms when they got off the bus. The candy was all gone by the time they got home. Their parents asked them, "Where are the fruits and vegetables?" They shrugged and said, "My TUMMY hurts."
